Dear Delegates

Please note that all the representatives will get multiple character and policy options to choose from in their background guides initially, so they will not necessarily be representing a particular house but rather a particular region.

This has been done to ensure a fair playing ground for every member in this committee, as this way the future course of the committee will largely depend on how you choose to represent your position given the situations in the realm, past and present. More details and explanation will of course follow in the guide.

Every delegate basically has the chance to determine the version of events depending on how they choose to act. Of course there will be conflicts of interests, arguments, clashes and maybe even wars. Alliances will be made or broken, Lords will be raised or removed, and anyone may at any time, die.

So are you ready? For winter is truly coming this year, especially for the chosen ones.
